INFORM Risk Index Framework

HAZARD

Natural

  • Drought
  • Flood
  • Earthquake
  • Cyclone/Storm
  • Wildfire

Human

  • Conflict
  • Violence Events

VULNERABILITY

Socio-Economic

  • Development/Poverty
  • Food Security
  • Dependency

Vulnerable Groups

  • Displaced Populations
  • Health Conditions
  • Child Mortality

COPING CAPACITY

Infrastructure

  • Health Services
  • Economic Capacity
  • Communication

Institutional

  • Governance
  • DRR Capacity
  • Early Warning

Risk Calculation Formula

RISK = HAZARD1/3 × VULNERABILITY1/3 × LACK_OF_COPING_CAPACITY1/3
